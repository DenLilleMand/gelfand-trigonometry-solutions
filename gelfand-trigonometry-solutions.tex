\begin{abstract}
\textit{Trigonometry} by Gelfand and Saul is often recommended as a precalculus text for self-study.
However, the lack of solutions for exercises can present difficulties for those who are self-studying.
A \href{https://jbeach50.weebly.com/gelfand--saul-trig-solutions.html}{partial set of solutions} for \textit{Trigonometry} (odd numbered exercises only) has been published by John Beach.
It is hoped that this document will eventually contain a complete set of solutions.
\end{abstract}
